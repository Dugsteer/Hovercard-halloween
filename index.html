Halloween Online Flashcards

Choose from a set of Halloween words such as bat, witch, cat and monster, or secretly enter your own word in this seasonally scary game that helps to practise spelling and the pronunciation of letters of the alphabet. Enter the letters one by one onto the big tombstone, but if you get a letter wrong, one of the little tombstones turns into a ghost and flies away. When there are none left — you lose!
